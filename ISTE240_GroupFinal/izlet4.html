Baska voda
A little bit about this destination...

Excerpt 1

Baska voda is a small town situated below the mountain of Biokovo, in Dalmatia, on the western part of Makarska Riviera. Throughout history this was a town of fisherman and merchants, which later became a modern tourist resort. Baska voda offers more than just relaxing resorts, and our students know that.

Excerpt 2

During our time there, we have visited the ancient military fort, the archeological museum and some of the richest archeological sites in the nearby town Gradina. The archeological site is filled with ancient Roman urns, jewelry and coins from the times of Alexander Severus Aurelianus.

Excerpt 3

After spending time learning something useful, we took our students for a walk on the Biokovo mountain with a tour guide. Students really enjoyed this trip because it was instructing and fun.
