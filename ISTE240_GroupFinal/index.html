About

Student Home Novi Zagreb is an educational institution that provides accommodation, nutrition and day-care for pupils attending secondary schools in the Zagreb and Velika Gorica areas. House capacity is 650 to 700 students who are deployed by educational groups. Each educational group has a teacher who cares for each individual student and for the educational group. The Student Home is Pavilion Type. There are 3 students in spacious rooms. The two rooms share a sanitary node, and each room has an Internet connection. There is also a library with all the necessary reading books, a computer classroom, a table tennis and chess room, an outdoor basketball and football court, and a gym for three days a week.In free time, students can organize and spend pleasant time in interesting activities that we will discuss more closely. In the House there is a kitchen and dining room where students have three meals a day. The following activities are offered at the House: football, basketball, table tennis for boys and girls, volleyball for boys and girls, chess for young men and girls, etc.

Location

The student home is located in Novi Zagreb, and it's the only student home in this part of town. Near the student home there are lots of sports and recreational facilities and artistic institutions as: Museum of contemporary arts, Zagrebački velesajam, park Bundek, Hipodrom, Cinestar, pools Utrine, Arena centar and commercial centre Avenue Mall. The student home's location provides fast and efficient communication and traffic connections to the bus and train station. The students using tram and bus lines quickly and timely get to their schools.

The student home address is Avenija Većeslava Holjevca 3, 10010 Zagreb
