Rijeka
A little bit about this destination...

Excerpt 1

Rijeka is the principal seaport and the third-largest city in Croatia, after Zagreb and Split. It is located in Primorje-Gorski Kotar County on Kvarner Bay, an inlet of the Adriatic Sea. Rijeka as a city has a great history, and many attractions to visit. Such as Trsat Castle, main street Korzo, and museums such as museum city of Rijeka.
