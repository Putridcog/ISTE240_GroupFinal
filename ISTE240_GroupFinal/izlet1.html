Varazdin
A little bit about this destination...

Excerpt 1

Varaždin is the city of baroque, young people, music, flowers and bicycles. Also called „Little Vienna“offers a rich natural legacy interwoven in parks, and a must see destination, located in the North of Croatia on the southern bank of the River Drava.

Excerpt 2

We visited Varazdin during the summer mainly because of Spancirfest. During Spancirfest, the city becomes the capital of music, the kingdom of theatre and realm of beauty created by the magic hands of artists. The streets and squares of the historic core, where the festival lives, become too tight. And tens of thousands of visitors ruled together with artists and musicians by those streets.

Excerpt 3
