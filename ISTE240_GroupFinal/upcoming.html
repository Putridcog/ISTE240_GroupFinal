LIPIK, DARUVAR

30/09/2018

About

Daruvar is located the central in the central part of Croatia, the western part of the province of Slavonia. It is located 125km from the capital Zagreb and about 130km from Osijek. It is located in a hilly area at the foot of the Papuk mountain, in the Poilean Plateau, on fertile grounds from the Pleistocene.
As a student home, we will visit various Daruvar sights, learn about Daruvar’s historic core, visit museums and talk to local people to get to know the city’s most important events there. Daruvar is a very interesting city, and we are certain that our students will love Daruvar’s spa.

Sports being provided

There is a playground near the hotel that we are staying in. So our students can enjoy the nice weather playing football, badminton, handball or just walking around the park nearby. Since this is a 4 day trip, our students will have plenty of time for outdoor activities.

Food

We will have organized lunch at the Flamingo restaurant which offers wide variety of foods that are typical for the Daruvar. Breakfast and dinner buffet will be at the hotel restaurant, so the students can take whatever food they like.

Accomodation

On this trip we will be staying for four days at the Hotel Mladimir. It's a nice hotel with reasonable room prices fit for everybody. The hotel has it's restaurant in which we'll be having breakfast and dinner. There is also a playground nearby, so our students will have free time in the outdoors.

CARNIVAL IN RIJEKA

29/02/2019

About

The carnival in Rijeka is held every year in February since 1982. It is the biggest carnival in Croatia, so we had to take our students to have a look. During the time of the carnival, there are organized carnival parades that are going through town. Each year there are many groups that have a same mask and walk through town greeting the carnival goers. The biggest event happened in 2001 when there were 144 group masks participating in the carnival, which were seen by more than 140 000 carnival goers.

Sports being provided

On this trip we are not providing any sports, mainly because we will spend the whole day strolling around the city center and enjoying the parades.

Food

We have organized lunch in the Gvardijan pizzeria. This restaurant offers lots of different pizzas as well as barbecue food, and also some vegan and vegetarian food. So we think this was the best choice because it offers something for everybody's taste.

Accomodation

On this trip we won't stay more than one day. So the accomodation is not necessary, since we are leaving Rijeka in the evening.
